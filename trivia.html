Marvel vs. DC — pass-and-play for 2–6 players

How to play

1

Add 2–6 players, pick a universe, and choose how many rounds.

2

Pass the phone to whoever's up. They tap Ready, then beat the clock on a multiple-choice question.

3

Each round bumps the difficulty — harder questions are worth more, and fast answers earn a speed bonus.

4

Highest total score after the final round earns the title of Multiverse Legend.
