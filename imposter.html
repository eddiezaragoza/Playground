Imposter
A pass-and-play party word game

How to play

1
Pass the device around.

Everyone sees their secret role privately. Most players get the same secret word — but one or more are the imposter and only see "IMPOSTER".

2
Drop one-word clues.

Take turns saying a single word that hints at the secret word. Imposters bluff — give a clue vague enough to fit in but specific enough to not get caught.

3
Vote out the imposter.

When you're ready, everyone votes for who they think is faking it. The most-voted player is eliminated.

4
Win or get caught.

If the imposter is caught, they get one chance to guess the secret word and steal the win. Otherwise — civilians take the round.
